KneuraLens™

How we look at AI governance.

The KneuraLens™ framework is the analytical lens we apply at every stage — four interlocking perspectives that together produce a complete picture of governance readiness.

I

Inventory Lens

A systematic view of every AI system — what it does, who owns it, what data it touches, and what would happen if it failed or was audited tomorrow.

  • System scope and function
  • Data flow and lineage
  • Ownership and accountability
  • Third-party dependencies
II

Risk Lens

Structured risk classification grounded in established frameworks — not gut feel. Every system rated against consistent criteria with documented rationale.

  • NIST AI RMF alignment
  • EU AI Act risk tier classification
  • Business impact analysis
  • Regulatory obligation mapping
III

Governance Lens

An assessment of the policies, structures, and processes that govern AI in your organisation — and where the gaps are.

  • Policy coverage assessment
  • Accountability gap analysis
  • Process maturity evaluation
  • Board and leadership visibility
IV

Readiness Lens

A forward-looking view of your organisation's capacity to maintain governance — and to demonstrate it to auditors, clients, and regulators.

  • Evidence pack quality review
  • Certification gap analysis
  • Team capacity assessment
  • Roadmap to audit-ready status
