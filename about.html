Our mission

Governance that is clear, defensible, and built to last.

Every mid-market business running AI deserves governance that is clear, defensible, and built to last.

Piyal Gupta spent fifteen years building AI risk frameworks for organisations with the infrastructure to do it right — dedicated compliance teams, enterprise legal departments, and frameworks written by specialists. Then he started working with mid-market companies. The AI exposure was identical. The regulatory pressure was identical. The governance infrastructure was not. Not because these companies didn't care — because nobody had built the right thing for them.

Kneuralabs was built to close that gap. We combine deep regulatory expertise in NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001, and the EU AI Act with a pragmatic, business-first approach to governance. We do not write policies that sit in drawers. We build governance programmes that run.
