About

We built Kneuralabs because
governance was being done wrong.

Mid-market companies were getting enterprise templates that didn't fit, consulting frameworks too complex to maintain, and advice disconnected from how organisations actually work. We decided to do it differently.

Mission

Structural AI governance for organisations that can't afford to get it wrong.

Kneuralabs was founded on a single conviction: that AI governance should be structural — built from the ground up for the organisation it's protecting, grounded in the standards that matter, and designed to survive long after the consultants have left.

We work exclusively with U.S. mid-market companies because that's where the governance gap is largest and the stakes are highest. These organisations have significant AI exposure but limited governance infrastructure. They face the same regulatory obligations as enterprises, with a fraction of the resources.

Our job is to close that gap — with precision, transparency, and work that holds up under scrutiny.

How We Work

Four principles. No exceptions.

01

Precision

Governance built on incomplete data or vague accountability structures eventually fails. We are precise about what we're assessing, what we're building, and what it's designed to withstand.

02

Transparency

We tell clients what we find — including when it's uncomfortable. Governance work that avoids difficult truths produces frameworks that don't protect anyone.

03

Defensibility

Every output we produce is designed to be defended — to regulators, auditors, boards, and clients. If we can't articulate the rationale, it doesn't go in the deliverable.

04

Sustainability

Governance frameworks that require consultants to maintain aren't governance frameworks. We design for the team you have, not the team we wish you had.
