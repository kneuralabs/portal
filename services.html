S1

AI System Inventory & Risk Classification

You can't govern what you can't see. S1 gives you a complete, auditable map of every AI system in your organisation — classified by risk level against the standards that matter.

We conduct structured interviews, technical reviews, and documentation analysis to produce an inventory that stands up to regulatory scrutiny. Every system is risk-classified against NIST AI RMF and EU AI Act criteria, with a clear rationale for each decision.

Timeline: 2–4 weeks Start S1
Deliverables
  • Complete AI system register with metadata
  • Risk classification matrix (NIST AI RMF aligned)
  • EU AI Act prohibited/high-risk system flagging
  • Third-party and vendor AI identification
  • Regulatory obligation mapping
  • Executive risk briefing document
Deliverables
  • AI governance policy suite (8–12 policies)
  • Accountability matrix with named role owners (RACI)
  • Incident detection and response protocol
  • AI lifecycle operating procedures
  • Procurement and third-party AI policy
  • Board-ready governance report and presentation
S2

AI Governance Framework Design

A governance framework built to last — not a template your team will abandon in six months. S2 produces a bespoke structure that fits your organisation, your risk profile, and your capacity.

Built on NIST AI RMF and ISO/IEC 42001, with explicit accountability structures and operating procedures written for your team — not a generic enterprise. Every policy is grounded in your actual AI inventory from S1.

Timeline: 4–8 weeks Start S2
S3

Standards Readiness & Certification Preparation

You've built the framework. Now you need to prove it. S3 takes you from internal governance to external certification — with structured preparation that makes the audit process tractable.

Gap analysis against your chosen standard (ISO/IEC 42001, NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, SOC 2 AI Trust Criteria), evidence collection, and a pre-audit readiness review conducted by practitioners who know what auditors are looking for.

Timeline: 4–8 weeks Start S3
Deliverables
  • Formal gap analysis report (standard-specific)
  • Evidence pack assembly and review
  • Control testing and documentation
  • Pre-audit readiness assessment
  • Remediation priority list with owners
  • Certification roadmap and timeline
What's Included
  • Monthly governance review sessions
  • New AI system onboarding support
  • Regulatory change monitoring and briefings
  • Incident response guidance
  • Annual framework refresh
  • On-call advisory access (email + async)
S4

Ongoing Governance Advisory

AI governance isn't a project. It's a function. S4 keeps your governance framework current as your AI footprint grows, regulations change, and new systems are deployed.

A retained advisory relationship — with structured monthly touchpoints, proactive regulatory monitoring, and on-call access when something unexpected happens. Designed to keep governance alive without building an internal team from scratch.
